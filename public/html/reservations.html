Reservations

Thank you for considering House of Hashimoto for your coming event

An integral part of the Raleigh community, HoH provides a variety of dining rooms perfect for business mettings, rehearsal dinners, birthdays, or anniversary parties.

Executive Chef Eichi Ito invites you to experience our rich, umami cuisine created from fresh, organic ingredients 100% sourced from Japan.







HoH provides 3 spaces available for private dining

Our "Sakura Room" seats 10 individuals and hosts 16 for a sake event

Our "Bonsai Room" seats 24 individuals and hosts 30 for a sake event

Our "Lotus Room" seats 50 individuals and hosts 60 for a sake event

***Note: Our "Main Hall" may be reserved for party sizes as high as 100


Booking Information

Please confirm final guest count and menu selection 7 days prior to the event

Charges will be based off of the final guest count or the guaranteed number, whichever is greater

A signed contract detailing the specifics of the event will be finalized 7 days before the event

Cancellations 1 day prior to event will be charged 50% of the food price

Cancellations the day of the event are responsible for 100% of the food price

All parties will be billed on a single check with an added 7.5% sales tax

We accept American Express, Visa, Master Card, and Discover Cards

Decorations are allowed, with exception to confetti and adhesives
